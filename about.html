About Us

At VedicShala, we bring the ancient wisdom of AYUSH right to your fingertips. Our Virtual Herbal Garden is designed to offer an immersive and educational journey into the world of medicinal plants.

Interactive Learning

Explore realistic 3D models of medicinal plants, allowing you to view them from every angle and gain a deeper understanding of their significance.

Comprehensive Information

We provide detailed insights on each plant, including its botanical and common names, habitat, medicinal uses, and cultivation methods.

Advanced Search Options

Easily find specific plants or filter them based on various criterias such as health, medicinal use, flowers, etc. ensuring a user-friendly experience.
